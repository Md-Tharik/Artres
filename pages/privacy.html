Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how ARTRES handles data.

No file uploads

ARTRES runs entirely in your browser. Any PDFs, images, or text you create or edit stay on your device. We do not upload, store, or process your files on any server.

No personal data collection

ARTRES does not require accounts or logins. We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, or passwords.

Analytics & cookies

ARTRES may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to understand overall usage (such as page views or device type). These analytics do not identify individual users.

Third-party services

ARTRES may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising platforms in the future. These services may use cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
